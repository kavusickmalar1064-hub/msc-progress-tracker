Entry Requirements

Minimum 2.2 Honours Bachelor Degree in Electronic, Computer or Communications Engineering or a related discipline. Applied Physics graduates and candidates with strong industrial experience may also be considered.

English language: Minimum IELTS 6.0 overall with no less than 6.0 in each component.

Career Opportunities

Graduates are equipped for high-level roles in research, design, and development within the telecommunications sector and the wider electronics industry — spanning RF engineering, embedded systems, photonics, VLSI design, and more.
